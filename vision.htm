Vision

Praise him for his mighty acts; praise him according to his excellent greatness!

Psalm 150

We want our praises to God to represent our best not only for the musicians up front but also for the congregation and for families, for the musically-talented and the musically-challenged. Regardless of our musical abilities, we all can learn to use and improve the instrument God gave us at birth.

The vision of Crescendo Software is to see believers worshipping God more excellently. Sing Your Part is an application to help people of all musical skills to learn to sing melodies and harmonies together.
