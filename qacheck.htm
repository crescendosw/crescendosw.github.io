Sing Your Part Quality Check

Thank you for helping check the accuracy of the songs in Sing Your Part. To get started, you will first need to install the app on an iPhone or iPad and then sign up using the instructions below.

Install Sing Your Part

If you already have Sing Your Part, great! You are finished with this step and can go ahead and Sign Up.

If you do not have Sing Your Part,
  1. Contact Susan Hewitt for a promo code for a free version.
  2. Go to the App Store and click the Apps icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll all the way down to the bottom until you see the Redeem button. Click it.
  4. Enter the promo code.

Sign Up

You will be signing up to check the vocal part for a block of 50 hymns in the Cantus 2020.

  1. Click here to sign up. We are using a Coda document for the signups and for keeping track of everyone's progress. You will need to create a Coda account if you do not already have one.
  2. Once you have successfully logged in, you will see a document with further instructions on how to sign up and report problems.

Start Testing

Now that you have installed Sing Your Part and have signed up, get out your Cantus 2020 and launch the app.

  1. Click on the Songs button at the top of the screen.
  2. Choose Cantus Christi 2020 from the list of hymnals.
  3. Scroll or search for your current hymn, which will be shown on the Song Checking Coda document you used to sign up.
  4. Open up your Cantus to the same hymn.
  5. Turn down the other parts and turn up your vocal part. Play the song while paying careful attention to the notes in the Cantus.
  6. If you find a problem, report it following the instructions on the Song Checking document.

FAQ

What kind of errors am I looking for?
Any kind of error. Here are examples:

  • incorrect accidentals
  • a correct note but the wrong octave
  • a default tempo that is way off
  • a note or rest held too long or to short
  • a tie that is played as two individual, repeated notes
  • the piano accompaniment plays instead of the vocal part
  • any other kind of musical error

Why are all the buttons on the sign-up sheet all greyed out and unresponsive?
You need to log in to Coda. You may have to create a Coda account.

What if it is a unison song?
The soprano should check the songs sung in unison. If you are an Alto, Tenor, or Bass, click the green Finished Checking Song button and go to the next song.
