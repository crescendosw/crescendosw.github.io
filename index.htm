Sing Your Part

The app making it easier to learn vocal parts for hymns.

Learning a vocal part often means needing access to an instrument and having the skills to play it. What is more, knowing a part does not always translate into being able to sing it with a group as that part gets lost in the other vocals and drowns in a sea of sound.

All that changes with Sing Your Part.

Learn a part

Listen to one vocal type and practice

Practice with other voices…
on your own

Increase or decrease the volume of other voices to practice singing your part while hearing the other parts. No need to coordinate practicing with others.  

Practice anywhere

We do not all have access to a piano or have the ability to play it. Learn while making dinner, in the dorm room, or on the go.
